Molecular Optimization with AI

Advanced Materials

Our founder was involved in the early days of abstracting & encoding molecular structures into neural networks - now widely spread using Graph Neural Networks. We reduced the experimental design of new eco-friendly fuels (e-Fuels) from several weeks to seconds. The AI optimization framework outputs tailored-made fuel that maximizes power output using only carbon-free components in the chemical structure.

Healthcare

Developed an automated segmentation system using U-Net architecture to analyze kidney vasculature from high-resolution 3D HiP-CT scans. The model achieves 97% accuracy, reducing segmentation time from months to hours per dataset. This supports the Vascular Common Coordinate Framework and Human Reference Atlas for medical research.
