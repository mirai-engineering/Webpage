Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical AI project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. Simple AI implementations can take 2-4 weeks, while comprehensive AIaaS solutions typically require 3-6 months. Our discovery phase (4-6 weeks) helps us provide accurate timelines and deliverables tailored to your specific requirements.

Do you provide ongoing support after deployment?

Yes, we offer comprehensive post-deployment support including model monitoring, performance optimization, and continuous improvement. Our AIaaS lifecycle includes ongoing maintenance, updates, and scaling services to ensure your AI solutions remain cutting-edge and deliver maximum value.

What industries do you specialize in?

We specialize in Engineering, Medical/Healthcare, Financial Technology, and Data Processing industries. Our expertise spans molecular optimization, medical imaging, real-time market forecasting, and AI agents. We work with companies of all sizes, from startups to enterprise organizations seeking to leverage AI for competitive advantage.

Can you work with our existing data infrastructure?

Absolutely! We design AI solutions that integrate seamlessly with your existing systems and data infrastructure. Our team has extensive experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP), on-premises deployments, and hybrid architectures. We ensure minimal disruption to your current operations while maximizing the value of your existing data assets.
